About Validate

Validate is a voucher generation and redemption platform that allows merchants to promote and advertise their business in a unique way. Validate is a complete validation platform allowing merchants to create and validate any form of voucher. Gift, deal, concession, pre paid, discount, and birthday vouchers can all be generated using validate as well as the generation and validation of ticketed events.
